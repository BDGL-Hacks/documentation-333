When you are going out for a night with friends you want to have fun. However, in order to have fun you would like to be sure that you can stay in touch with the friends in your group and also that your friends make it home safely. PartyUp is a new iOS app that helps you stay in touch with the people that matter. PartyUp lets you create an itinerary for your group so that everyone in the group knows where to go. During your night out, PartyUp keeps track of where you and your group members are in the itinerary so you can easily find someone if you lose track of them. PartyUp also promotes party safety with a "ping" feature that allows users to check whether their group members are okay with a single tap. PartyUp is your hub for simple group communication and fluid event planning.

We were unable to set to TestFlight for our app in time, so we have included all of the necessary files to run the app from Xcode. In our src directory, there are two subdirectories that contain iOS code: frontend_src and frontend_full. frontend_full contains everything that Xcode needs to run our app, while frontend_src contains only the source files that we wrote throughout the semester.
